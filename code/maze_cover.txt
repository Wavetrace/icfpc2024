Push starting point L to a stack.
curr pos = pos of L.
while (stack not empty) {
  pop top of stack, call it T.
  record move to T from curr pos.
  curr pos = T.
  Push all nonvisited accessible neighbors of T onto stack, mark as in-stack
  Mark T as visited.
}
